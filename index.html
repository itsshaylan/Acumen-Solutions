Interchange Plus

Best for:

  • Majority card-present, swiped transaction
  • High number of transactions per month
  • Average ticket amount ranges from $1 - $500; average ticket around $100
  • Most common card types are: Debit, Visa, MasterCard & Discover (non-rewards type cards, such as travel rewards, cash back, restaurant rewards, etc. Think Capital One brand cards are all rewards card, but they are backed by MasterCard, they are more expensive to process).
  • Having more control to proactively lower monthly payment processing expenses is important to the business owner

Industry Types:

  • Retail
  • Restaurants
  • Small dollar, recurring subscriptions (gym memberships, etc)
  • Gas stations & convenience stores
  • Appointment-based services, such as, doctor’s offices, dental, vets, spa, nail salons, hairdressers, municipalities, pest treatment services, etc.

Flat Rate

Best for:

  • Majority card not present, non-swiped transactions (manually keyed, over the phone, etc.)
  • Low number of transactions per month
  • Average ticket amount is over $500
  • Most common card types are: American Express and/or rewards cards. Even if it’s a 50/50 split between AMEX/rewards & debit/VISA/MC/DISC the savings in flat rate on the AMEX/rewards is revealed
  • Accurately forecasting monthly payment processing expenses is important to the business owner

Industry Types:

  • Service-based companies like HVAC, plumbers, electricians, auto repairs, handymen & home repair (high ranges in ticket amounts, lots of manually keyed in sales)
  • Equipment sales
  • Business to business sales
  • Donations, non-profits

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a number to call after hours for technical support?

Our helpdesk is available 24/7. If you need assistance after office hours, call our help desk at 1-800-864-4644. You will be prompted to leave a message including your merchant ID number and a return phone number. A technician will return your call as quickly as possible.

How do I start accepting Amex and/or Discover?

A New Amex or Discover Application form will need to be completed, signed by the account signatory and faxed in.

When can I expect to receive my deposits?

You can expect to see your deposits within 2 business days after they are settled.

I need help understanding my rates, and reading my statement.

If you need an explanation of your rates and statement, please contact our help desk at 1-800-864-4644 and we will be happy to assist you.
